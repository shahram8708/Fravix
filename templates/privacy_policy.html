Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us at Fravix E-Library. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information, ensuring full transparency and trust in our practices.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information when you register, browse, or use our services:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, enrollment number, and any other details you provide during registration.
  • Usage Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, and access times may be automatically collected for analytical and security purposes.
2. Use of Information

Your personal information will be used for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To improve the user experience by understanding your preferences and usage patterns.
  • Communication: To send you important updates, notifications, or service-related announcements.
  • Security: To ensure the integrity of your account and maintain a safe and secure platform.
3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Secure access controls and procedures to prevent unauthorized usage of your account.
  • Regular audits and system checks to ensure data security.
4. Sharing of Information

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. Your personal information will never be sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except in the following situations:

  • If required by law or to comply with legal proceedings.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Fravix E-Library and its users.
5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These small data files allow us to personalize your experience and track user behavior on our site. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. User Rights & Control

As a user, you have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any privacy concerns, please reach out to us via our Contact Us page.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via email or on the platform. By continuing to use our services, you accept the updated terms.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please do not hesitate to Contact Us. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
